PERSON OF THE WEEK

Lisa Dziuba is one of the co-founders of Flawless App, a tool for iOS developers to compare the expected design with the real app inside the dev environment. She strongly believes in bringing design and development closer together and shares her knowledge as a speaker, blogger, and mentor. Lisa maintains a blog for the iOS community and helps others find the right resources to solve practical marketing and design tasks by curating “Marketing for Engineers” and “Awesome Design Tools”. To help connect the iOS/OSX community, she is also part of the CocoaHeads Ukraine team.
